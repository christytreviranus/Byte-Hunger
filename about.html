Did You Know?


  • Each year about 63 million tons of food is wasted annually in the U.S.

  • The restaurant industry alone generates about 11.4 million tons of food waste annually at a cost of about $25 billion per year

  • For every dollar invested in food-waste reduction, restaurants can realize about $8 in cost savings

  • Preventing waste in restaurants could save nearly $620 million annually, and tracking food that is thrown away could cut food costs by 2 percent to 6 percent

  • Only 10 percent of surplus food is being recovered in the foodservice and restaurant sector, leaving about 1.5 billion meals per year uneaten
